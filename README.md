## Cris A. Fugate (Python Developer, Backend and Data Processing 🐍) - Freelancing

I have experience as a backend developer, but my focus is data processing with tools like Pandas, SQL, NoSQL, Streamlit, Openpyxl.<br>
I have used Pandas at my previous two fulltime jobs to collect, compute, and layout data for reports.<br>
I have used SQL at my previous two fulltime jobs to (1) collect data for insurance reports, (2) collect data for billing reports.<br>
I have used Openpyxl at my second previous fulltime job to automate the creation of Excel reports from complex SQL and NoSQL (MongoDB) queries.<br>
I have used Streamlit and JupyterLab on personal projects to build dashboards and interactive AI interfaces.<br>

My previous three fulltime jobs have involved (not solely) building data pipelines to either (1) extract, clean, and load data into Django, (2) query, clean, and create complex Excel reports, or (3) query, compare, compute, and create CSV reports. In 2024 I couldn't find a job, so I started freelancing. I thought that I was going to do real estate tech support, but the realtor fell ill, and I couldn't find another realtor who wanted support.<br>
